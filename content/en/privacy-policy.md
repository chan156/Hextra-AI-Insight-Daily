### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking technologies used include beacons, tags, and scripts for collecting and tracking info, and to improve and analyze our Service. The technologies we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file dropped on your Device. You can tell your browser to refuse all Cookies or to clue you in when a Cookie is being sent. However, if you don't play along and accept Cookies, you might not be able to use certain parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser setting to refuse Cookies, our Service might just go ahead and use Cookies.

- **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These allow the Company to, for example, count users who have visited those pages or opened an email and for other related website statistics (like noting the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. When you're offline, Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device, while Session Cookies get the boot the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes laid out below:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital to give you services available through the Website and to let you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and stop any fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're cruising around the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

For more info about the Cookies we use and your options regarding Cookies, please visit our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
